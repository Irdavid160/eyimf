About EYIMF

Examine Yourselves International Mission Foundation exists to glorify the Lord Jesus Christ through Gospel-centered evangelism, discipleship, and practical compassion. By God’s grace, we serve local Filipino communities with food, clothing, and by strengthening the Church through the Word of God.

Our Mission

To proclaim Christ, equip believers with an understanding of Scripture, and mobilize compassionate action—so that families, churches, and communities are transformed for the glory of God.

Our Vision

A God-glorifying Church in every community, disciples multiplying disciples, and lives transformed by the gospel.

What We Do

We serve through local evangelism and discipleship, practical compassion (food, clothing, community projects), and supporting local churches with biblical training and resources.

Ministries

Children's Ministry

Weekly children's church programs, Bible teaching, and resources for families.

Pastor & leader training, Bible study groups, and mentoring for new believers.

Relief distributions, feeding programs, and practical aid for needy families.

Video teachings, devotions, and resources to strengthen local churches online and offline.

Our Story

Never in a million years did I imagine I would one day be a missionary in the Philippines. Though I’ve always had a heart for missions and supported many missionaries over the years, I never intended to serve long-term in a foreign country. But when I first visited the Philippines, I not only found the love of my precious soulmate; I also fell in love with the wonderful people of her homeland.

What began as a plan to bring Myrna to America became God’s plan to bring me here. I wrestled with this calling more than anything the Lord has ever asked of me. My life back home is sweet. I’m very close to my family, immediate and extended, and being part of the largest family in the state of Ohio means there are many loved ones to miss. My children and grandchildren are my world, and I treasure every moment with them.

But I came to understand something deeply: God’s work is more important than anything else in this life. I don’t see my obedience as a loss, I see it as an investment in eternity. I believe God will give me forever with my family, and I long for the day when I can introduce them to those I’ve led to Christ from this mission field. My desire isn’t for wealth, fame, or power. My greatest joy will be to enter Heaven with a multitude of souls won for the glory of His Kingdom.
