About EYIMF

Examine Yourselves International Mission Foundation exists to glorify the Lord Jesus Christ through Gospel-centered evangelism, discipleship, and practical compassion. By God’s grace, we serve local Filipino communities—feeding the hungry, clothing the poor, and strengthening the Church through the Word of God.

Our Mission

To proclaim Christ, equip believers with an understanding of Scripture, and mobilize compassionate action—so that families, churches, and communities are transformed for the glory of God.

Our Vision

A growing network of Scripture-anchored believers, house churches in every barangay (neighborhoods), and local leaders who make disciples, care for the vulnerable, and carry the Gospel across the islands and beyond.

What We Do

Teach the Word

Preaching, small home fellowships, and discipleship built on the Word of God, with a focus on faith, obedience and spiritual growth.

Feed the Hungry

Food distributions organized with our local home churches and leaders—meeting urgent needs while sharing the hope of Christ.

Clothe the Poor

Dignified clothing outreaches that honor every person as made in the image of God.

Youth & Training

Equipping the next generation in Bible study, evangelism, service, and godly character.

Compassion Ministries

Visitation, prayer, and practical help for families, widows, and the elderly.

Community Stewardship

Grassroots initiatives (including recycling) that support livelihood and care for creation.

Where We Serve

EYIMF will partner with local believers and community leaders across the Philippines. We prioritize long-term relationships, accountability, and local leadership development in every community we serve.

Our Story

What began as simple obedience to the Great Commission has grown into a fellowship of believers committed to Scripture, prayer, and practical service. The Lord has opened doors for discipleship gatherings, family outreach, and mercy ministries—always grounded in the Gospel.

Statement of Faith

  • The Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.
  • There is one God, eternally existent in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in the finished work of the Lord Jesus Christ.
  • True faith produces good works as defined by the Word of God, as the evidence of a transformed life in Christ.
  • The Church is called to make disciples of all nations, teaching them to observe all that Christ commanded.

Leadership & Accountability

EYIMF operates with pastoral counsel, financial oversight, in America and local leadership on the ground. We aim to steward every gift with integrity, transparency, and thanksgiving to God.

How You Can Partner

  • Pray: for open doors, faithful workers, and lasting fruit.
  • Serve: share skills, translate resources, or help organize local outreaches.
  • Give: provide food packs, Bibles, and support for discipleship and mercy ministries.
