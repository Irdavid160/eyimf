About Examine Yourselves International Mission Foundation

“Examine yourselves, whether ye be in the faith; prove your own selves.” — 2 Corinthians 13:5 (KJV)

We are a Christ-centered nonprofit serving communities in the Philippines through Bible teaching and practical care. Our mission is simple: point people to Jesus while meeting tangible needs with integrity and love.

Our Distinctives

  • Scripture-first ministry: the Gospel is central to everything we do.
  • Local empowerment: we partner with local leaders and churches.
  • Transparent stewardship: clear reporting and faithful accountability.
