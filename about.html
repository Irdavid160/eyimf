About EYIMF

Examine Yourselves International Mission Foundation serves communities with Gospel-centered discipleship and practical care. Our heart is to proclaim Christ, train believers, and mobilize compassionate action.

Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, equip the saints through Scripture, and serve the poor with dignity—so that Christ would be glorified.

Vision

Churches planted, families strengthened, and a new generation of disciplined, joyful servants raised up to carry the Gospel.

Statement of Faith

  • The Bible (KJV) is the inspired, inerrant Word of God.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • The Church is called to make disciples and serve the poor.
