What We Do

By God’s grace, EYIMF serves communities through Gospel-centered discipleship and practical compassion. Below are our core ministry areas and some stories you can explore and share.

Children's Church

Teaching God's Word in the simplest form for children.

Feed the Hungry

Food packs for families in need, with prayer and Scripture encouragement.

Clothe the Poor

Dignified clothing distributions led by local partners.

Youth & Training

Equipping young believers in Bible study, evangelism, and service.

Compassion Ministries

Visitation, prayer, and practical care for families, widows, and the elderly.

Community Stewardship

Grassroots projects (incl. recycling) that support livelihood and care for creation.

Children's Church

Our children’s church was born out of a simple burden: to teach God’s Word to children in a way that they can understand and cherish. We began gathering under a small covered area, sharing Bible stories, singing songs, and introducing Scripture one verse at a time.

Over time, this grew into a weekly children’s ministry where young hearts are learning the Gospel, memorizing Scripture, and discovering the love of Christ.
