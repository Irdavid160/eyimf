Ministry Goals

Year 1 Goals (Nov 2025 – Nov 2026)

  • Establish weekly Sunday services in our home church.
  • Launch Saturday teen outreach.
  • Continue Sunday’s children’s church.
  • Begin Saturday ministry training classes for future leaders.
  • Provide 36 families per week with food packages (goal: 70 by year-end).
  • Distribute 50 parallel Tagalog/KJV Bibles six times per year.
  • Prepare for annual clothing and toy shipments for distribution.
  • Begin groundwork for a second barangay church by August 2026.

🇺🇸 Calling U.S. Volunteers

Help Us Bring Hope to the Philippines Christmas 2026

God has placed a great vision on our hearts — to bless the families and children in the Philippines with clothing, toys, and personal care items in time for Christmas 2026. To make this vision a reality, we need volunteers across the United States who are willing to organize, collect, and help ship these gifts of love.

This will be a nationwide effort involving local churches, regional collection centers, and one central U.S. shipment point. Here’s how it will work:

🏠 Local Volunteers

We’re asking individuals and church groups to lead local collection efforts in their communities, gathering clothing, toys, and personal care products throughout the coming year. Each volunteer or group will serve as a collection point within their local assembly, encouraging others to give from their abundance.

🚚 Regional Coordinators

Local volunteers will transport their collected items to regional collection sites. Each regional site will be responsible for storing, organizing, and preparing these items for shipment to the central U.S. collection point.

We’re also seeking regional leads who can communicate with local volunteers and help coordinate logistics in their area.

⚓ Central Coordination Team

We are seeking one or more lead coordinators who will:

  • Communicate with the regional teams
  • Work with the shipping company to arrange the container shipment
  • Oversee logistics to ensure all items arrive at the port on time

🗓 Project Timeline

  • Volunteer roles filled by: February 1, 2026
  • All regional collections completed by: August 15, 2026
  • Container shipped by: September 1, 2026
  • Arriving in the Philippines by: November 2026 (for Christmas distribution)

💗 Our Mission

This is more than a humanitarian project — it is an opportunity to show the love of Christ in action. Every box packed, every toy donated, and every mile traveled will carry the message that God sees, God cares, and God provides.

“Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works, and glorify your Father which is in heaven.” — Matthew 5:16

🙌 How You Can Get Involved

If you or your church would like to be part of this effort, please reach out to us. Whether you can help collect, coordinate, or connect us with others who can, your role matters.

Together, we can fill this container — not just with goods, but with grace, generosity, and the Gospel.
