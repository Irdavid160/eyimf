What Is EYIMF?

Examine Yourselves International Mission Foundation is a Christ-centered ministry devoted to inspiring believers worldwide to examine their hearts, align their lives with God’s Word, and serve others through both spiritual and practical means. EYIMF seeks to unite churches and volunteers across nations to share the Gospel, provide aid, and strengthen communities through faith-driven action and discipleship.
